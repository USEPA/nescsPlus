Select checkboxes for the classes and subclasses to be included in your query. Click on "Environmental [Sub-]Classes" and "Ecological End-product Classes" to see checkboxes for each of these two components. Farther down, you can choose to select from either the "Direct Use/Non-use AND Direct User" classes or from "Beneficiary" classes. After finalizing your selections through the checkboxes, click on the "Query Data" button to view the results of your query. If you would like to download the results to a spreadsheet file, click on the "Export CSV" button.

Where?
Environment classes and subclasses are the spatial environments where each ecological end-product is located when used.
What?
Ecological End-Product Classes are the relevant biophysical components of nature that are directly used or appreciated by humans.
